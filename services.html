Brand Identity

Your brand is not a logo. It's the sum of every impression you leave — the texture of your packaging, the cadence of your copy, the weight of your typeface. I build comprehensive visual identity systems that are coherent, distinctive, and designed to grow with you.

I work best with founders and brand teams who understand that great identity is an investment, not an expense — and who want a design partner they can trust to make brave decisions.

My Process

01

Discovery

We begin with a deep-dive conversation about your brand, your audience, your competitors, and your ambitions. I listen more than I talk.

02

Strategy

Before any design begins, I develop a clear strategic brief — your positioning, your visual language principles, and the emotional territory your brand should occupy.

03

Design

I work in focused sprints, presenting two or three distinct creative directions before refining the chosen route to the highest level of craft.

04

Delivery

All files, guidelines, and assets are delivered in organised, production-ready formats. I stay available for questions during the implementation phase.
