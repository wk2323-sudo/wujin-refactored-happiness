Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

How accurate is the Health Identity quiz? +

Our 8-question quiz uses a weighted multi-factor model that maps you to one of 16 archetypes. Internal validation shows 89% alignment with extended in-app behavioral analysis after 30 days of tracking.

Can my Health Identity actually change? +

Yes — that's the point. Your identity adapts as your sleep, diet, stress, and exercise patterns evolve. Most users shift archetypes once or twice per semester.

Where do the supplements come from? +

All packs are curated from third-party-tested brands including Thorne, Garden of Life, and iHerb. Every product is GMP-certified and ingredient-traceable.

Can I pause or cancel my subscription? +

Anytime, no questions asked. Pause for a month if you're traveling or studying abroad — your dashboard remembers your identity and picks back up when you return.

Is my data private? +

Yes. Multimodal AI analysis runs on-device wherever possible, and we never sell or share personal health data. You can export or delete everything from your profile settings.

Do you ship outside the U.S.? +

Currently we ship to the U.S. and Canada. International access is rolling out by region in 2026 starting with the UK, Australia, and South Korea.

What if my pack doesn't work for me? +

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on your first pack. If you don't feel a difference, message us and we'll re-tune your identity assessment or refund you in full.
